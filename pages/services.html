How Our Services Work

All flights are led by an FAA Part 107 certified pilot with insured operations and a scoped pre-flight plan. Our base fee is $150, which includes up to 1 hour on-site. Additional time is billed at $50 per 30 minutes. Services requiring professional photo or video editing (such as real estate listings, events, and commercial projects) include editing time in the quoted price. If you do not see exactly what you need, reach out and we'll tailor a package for you.
