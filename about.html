About EAZ Drones

Owner-operated aerial services built on an engineering foundation.

Meet the Owner

I'm the founder, Remote Pilot in Command, and sole operator behind EAZ Drones. When you reach out, you speak directly with the person who will plan, fly, and deliver your project; there are no middlemen and no hand-offs.

I'm currently pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering at the University of West Florida, where I've developed a rigorous, systems-level approach to problem-solving. That same engineering mindset drives every flight: meticulous pre-flight checks, safety measures, and data-driven workflows that produce consistent, professional results.

Why Work With Me

  • Direct Communication: You deal directly with the decision-maker from the initial quote through final delivery.
  • Technical Rigor: An engineering education means precise planning, reliable execution, and documented processes.
  • Community Focus: I built EAZ Drones to serve homeowners, realtors, and businesses across the Florida Panhandle and Emerald Coast, which is my community.
  • Fully Insured & FAA-Compliant: Every operation follows Part 107 regulations and is covered by comprehensive liability insurance.

Mission

Deliver high-quality aerial imagery and reliable inspection data to my neighbors and local businesses, delivering projects on time, on budget, and with the professionalism they deserve.

Values

Safety, precision, transparency, and genuine customer care.
